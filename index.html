Interesting facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are a very unique branch of the lizard group of reptiles.
  2. The chameleons' eyes can rotate and focus separately on 180-degree arcs, so they can see two different objects at the same time. This gives them a foll 360-degree field of vision.
  3. Chameleons are not deaf but they do not actually have ear openings.

Interesting facts about Camel

  1. Camels travel in herds and both dromedary and Bactrian camels are social creatures. Groups are composed of as many as 30 individuals including a family unit with one dominant male.
  2. When a camel does come across water, it can fill up in a hurry, drinking as much as 26 gallons in 10 minutes
  3. Camel's milk is lower in cholesterol and higher in vitamin C and minerals like sodium and potassium compared with the milk of other ruminants

Interesting facts about Teddy

  1. The Teddy Bears’ Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step

Interesting facts about Panda

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  2. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)
  3. Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day

Interesting facts about Cicada

  1. All cicada species in North America came from a common ancestor
  2. cicada species switches from being on a 13-year cycle to a 17-year cycle

Interesting facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.
